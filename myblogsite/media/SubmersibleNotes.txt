submersible regulation

- Title 46 CFR Subchapter T (parts 175-187)
- 33 U.S.C. 1221
- Less than 6 passengers is considered an "uninspected vessel" (46 U.S.C. 2101(42)
	- Must meet 46 CFR Subchapter C - Uninspected Vessels
	- 33 CFR Part 155 - Oil Pollution
	- 33 CFR Part 159 - Marine Sanitation Devices
	- 33 CFR Subchapter S - Boating Safety
	- 46 CFR part 15 - Manning Requirements
	- Can only be permitted in U.S. passeger operations if designed and constructed to a recognized industry standard.
- All operations manuals and vessel designs must be approeved by the Commanding Officer, MSC
	- If vessel has pressure hull of design other than that permitted by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME), the proposal will be forwarded by the MSC to COmmandant (G-MTH-4)
